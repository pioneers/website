Who Are We?

Pioneers in Engineering (PiE) promotes science, technology, engineering, and mathematics (STEM) education through an exciting, mentorship-based process. We strongly believe that no student should be denied a quality STEM education experience.

PiE on the Road is a traveling workshop developed by Pioneers in Engineering and hosted at local high schools to bring a new STEM experience to a high school near you.
